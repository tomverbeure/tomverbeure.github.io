

Backside removal:

* Figure 3-9, 3-15
* PDF page 71


* Remove 10 screws to remove top cover
* Remove 10 screws to remove bottom cover
* Put unit on front handles
* Remove 6 screws on left and right side to remove back side (but don't remove it yet!)
* Disconnect 3 top back connectors washers (red, yellow, MOD in marked)
* Disconnect backside RF Out SMA connector
* Disconnect 2 bottom back connectors washers (REF IN, REF OUT)
* Remove the screws to disconnect the OCXO.
* The backside is still locked into place by 2 pegs that side in a hole. 
  Put the unit flash. Push on the peg and pull out the backside. 
  Then do it on the other side.
* The backside is now loose, but there are still cables
* Unplug the IEEE flat cable
* Unplug the SYN and the OUT power cables
* Unplug the digital IDC power cable
* The power supply is now completely disconnected.
* Remove 6 screws to remove the power supply cover

* Input rectifier board A5A4: Voltage between E1 and E2: 165V. Between E3 and E2: -165V.
* Lamp DS1 sits across E1 and E3. It glows when there's voltage across (and it also
  helps discharging.) It glows long after disconnecting the main power.
